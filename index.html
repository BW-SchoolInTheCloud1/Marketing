Closing the achievement gap by connecting students with available qualified volunteer mentors.

HOW IT WORKS

Meet the requirements

You must be 18 years or older, and commit to at least one hour per week.

Register Online

Follow a few simple steps in the registration process, including, scheduling an orientation and choosing a school site.

Orientation

Meet staff and other volunteers, and learn more about being a tutor.

Background Check

Complete a background check and submit the information.

Get Started

Schedule a session time. Once you're matched with a student you can begin tutoring!

Make an impact

By following a highly effective, structured, and easy-to-use curriculum, students build confidence and make impressive gains.
